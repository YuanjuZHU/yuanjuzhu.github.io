Cage-based deformation in Virtual Reality(2020)

Changes in 3D models require to loop back to the authoring tool for performing the wished modification and the successive adaptation processes. Shape modification capabilities within the virtual environment can avoid the above modification-adaptation loop.
We present our first step in the development of a 3D modelling system in Virtual Reality. The shape modification is achieved through a cage-based deformation approach, applied to semantically enriched meshes, carrying annotated meaningful regions, thus allowing the direct selection and editing of significant object parts.

Visualization of Material growth in PVD process(2019)

Physical vapor deposition (PVD) is a process that forms a thin film on a component, which can be used in the chip manufacturing industry.
This game aims to visualize the distribution of atoms in the coating (crystal) produced by the PVD process, and gives the cross-section and edge of the cross-section at different growth heights.
The data comes from Professor Aurélien BESNARD of the École nationale supérieure d'Arts et Métiers.
